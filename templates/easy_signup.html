Our Welcome to You

We would like to officially welcome you to our club! We love everything tech related, from computers, to robots, to the cloud! We are a student-run group determined to help build and mold the creativity and maturity of anyone interested in electronics or technology, from all disciplines and skillsets. Some things we have planned for the upcoming year are:

  • UB Hacking 2016 - A fun Hackathon to build whatever you can in 24 hours!
  • Python workshop - For beginners and intermediate programmers alike
  • Golang workshop - For people who are new to the language.
  • Resume workshops - For technical-minded people trying to write a resume.
  • Interview workshops - For technical-minded people trying to pass an interview.
